# Nova Academy — Website Instructions

## 🎯 Primary Goals (Priority-Based)

1. Generate leads (WhatsApp + Calls)
2. Drive direct calls
3. Build trust and credibility

⚠️ Note:
- This is NOT an e-commerce website
- No course selling or payments required

---

## ⚡ Core User Action (Within 10 Seconds)

User should:
- Call directly
OR
- Message on WhatsApp

All design decisions should support this.

---

## 🧠 Positioning

Nova Academy is NOT just another coaching center.

Key Differentiators:
- Premium ambience (visually superior environment)
- Small batch sizes → personalized attention
- Higher quality education vs local competitors

---

## 👥 Target Audience

Primary:
- Parents of students (Nursery to 10th)

Secondary:
- College students (upskilling)
- Class 10–12 students (skill-based learning)

---

## 💭 Audience Pain Points

- Child cannot study at home
- Easily distracted
- Lack of structured guidance
- No personal attention in large batches

---

## 🗣️ Language & Tone

- Language: English
- Tone: Premium institute (not casual, not overly friendly)
- Avoid slang or Hinglish

---

## 🧱 Website Structure

### Pages:

1. Home
2. Courses
3. About
4. Contact
5. Results & Testimonials
6. Gallery
7. Blog
8. FAQ

---

## 📚 Course Segmentation

Clearly separate:

### 1. School Coaching
- Nursery to Class 10

### 2. Upskilling Courses
- Digital Marketing
- Web Design & Development
- Graphic Design

---

## 📲 Conversion System

### Primary CTAs:
- “Call Now”
- “Chat on WhatsApp”

### Secondary CTAs:
- “Book a Demo Class”
- “Free Trial Class”

⚠️ These should appear:
- Above the fold
- Sticky (header or floating button)
- Repeated across sections

---

## 🧩 Required Features

- Testimonials section (even if curated)
- Blog section
- FAQ section
- Gallery (real images of academy)

---

## 🚫 Exclusions

- No online payments
- No login/dashboard system
- No course hosting

---

## 🎨 UX Guidelines (Important)

- Fast loading (no heavy clutter)
- Clean spacing → premium feel
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Minimal but bold sections
- Mobile-first design

---

## 🧠 Design Psychology

- Parents should feel: “This is structured and reliable”
- Students should feel: “This looks modern and high quality”

---

## 🔥 Non-Negotiables

- Clear CTA within first screen
- Visual proof (images of academy)
- No generic coaching language
- Avoid clutter and overcrowding
