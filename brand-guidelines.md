# Nova Academy — Brand Guidelines

---

## 🧠 Brand Core

### Meaning:
“Nova” represents transformation, brilliance, and growth.

### Motto:
Tradition in Excellence

---

## 💎 Brand Personality

- Premium
- Structured
- Aspirational

This is NOT a casual tuition brand.
This is a disciplined, high-quality academy.

---

## 🎯 Brand Perception Goal

When someone lands on the website, they should think:

“This is a premium academy… but still affordable.”

---

## 🎨 Color Palette

### Primary Colors:

- Navy Blue → Trust, authority, education
- Beige → Warmth, balance, premium softness

### Usage:

- Navy Blue → Backgrounds, headings, buttons
- Beige → Sections, highlights, contrast areas

⚠️ Avoid:
- Bright/random colors
- Overuse of gradients
- Cheap-looking combinations

---

## 🔤 Typography

### Style:
Bold + Edgy + Modern Sans Serif

### Guidelines:

- Headings → Bold, strong presence
- Body text → Clean, readable, slightly spaced
- Avoid decorative or “school-like” fonts

---

## 🖼️ Imagery Style

### Use:

- Real academy photos
- Clean, well-lit classrooms
- Students engaged in learning

### Avoid:

- Stock images (as much as possible)
- Overly staged visuals
- Low-quality images

---

## 🗣️ Brand Voice

### Tone:

- Confident
- Clear
- Future-focused

### Style:

- “Let’s build your future tomorrow”
- Motivational but not cheesy
- Professional, not robotic

---

## ✍️ Copy Guidelines

### DO:

- Focus on outcomes (focus, discipline, growth)
- Speak to parents’ concerns
- Keep sentences clean and direct

### DON'T:

- Use generic lines like:
  - “Best coaching institute”
  - “We provide quality education”

---

## 🧲 Trust Builders

- Testimonials (even if curated)
- Real photos of academy
- Clean layout (premium = trust)

---

## 🧩 UI Personality

- Minimal but bold
- Spacious layouts
- Strong sections
- Smooth interactions

---

## ⚡ Brand Rule

If it looks like a “local tuition center” → reject it.

Everything should feel:
Structured. Premium. Intentional.
