Admissions & Enrollment

What are the primary criteria for student selection? expand_more
Nova Academy seeks students who demonstrate not only academic potential but a unique curiosity for cross-disciplinary learning. Our selection process involves a comprehensive review of prior academic records, a personal narrative submission, and a structured interview to ensure alignment with our collaborative culture.
Are there specific intake periods for international students? expand_more
We operate on a dual-intake system. The primary Autumn term begins in September, and a limited cohort is accepted for the Winter term starting in January. We recommend international applicants begin their dossier preparation at least six months prior to their intended start date.

Academics & The 1:15 Ratio

How is the 1:15 ratio maintained across all disciplines? expand_more
Our institutional charter mandates a strict ceiling for classroom occupancy. This ensures that every scholar receives personalized mentorship and allows our faculty to tailor curricula to individual learning trajectories. In specialized laboratory settings, this ratio often drops further to 1:8.
Does the academy support vocational or purely theoretical paths? expand_more
We believe in the "Applied Ledger" philosophy. While theoretical mastery is non-negotiable, every academic pathway includes a practicum component where scholars apply their knowledge to real-world industrial or social challenges.

Campus & Facilities

What security protocols are in place for the residential campus? expand_more
Security is paramount. Nova Academy employs a non-intrusive biometric access system and 24/7 professional concierge services. Our campus is a "walled garden" that balances complete safety with the freedom for students to explore and collaborate.

Post-Graduation Pathways

How does the academy facilitate University placements? expand_more
Our dedicated 'Future Office' begins working with students in their penultimate year. We have direct placement pipelines with top-tier global universities and provide specialized coaching for entrance examinations and personal portfolio development.
