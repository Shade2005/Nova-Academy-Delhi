# DESIGN.md - Nova Academy

## Brand Core
- Meaning: Transformation, brilliance, and growth.
- Motto: Tradition in Excellence

## Brand Personality
- Premium, Structured, Aspirational. Not a casual tuition brand but a disciplined academy.

## Section 6 (Design System Notes for Stitch Generation)

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
*   **Aesthetics Effect:** Premium, aspirational, highly structured. Do not make it look like a cheap local tuition center.
*   **Colors:** Primary elements: Navy Blue (Trust, authority, backgrounds, headings, buttons). Secondary elements: Beige (Warmth, balance, sections, highlights). Avoid overly bright/random colors and overuse of gradients.
*   **Typography:** Bold, Edgy, Modern Sans Serif for headings. Clean and slightly spaced for body text. No decorative or default school-like fonts.
*   **Imagery:** Professional academy images, clean classrooms, students engaged. No cheesy stock images.
*   **UI/UX:** Minimal but bold. Spacious layouts, strong sections. The layout should have a sense of structure. Fast loading.
*   **Copy/Tone:** Confident, clear, and future-focused. Drive outcomes (focus, discipline).
*   **Navigation & CTAs:** Extremely critical. "Call Now" and "Chat on WhatsApp" must be sticky/primary. All interactions must push towards direct communication. Secondary CTAs can be "Book a Demo Class" or "Free Trial Class".
